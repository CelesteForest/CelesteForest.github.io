Neural Controlled Bionic Hand

CAD Design 3D Printing Electrical Circutry

I designed a 3D‑printed neural prosthetic arm capable of measuring electromyographic signals from a user. I owned the CAD, mechanical design, and manufacturing of the project. Delivered within an accelerated two‑week timeframe, the project demanded rapid iterative prototyping, efficient integration of mechanical and electrical systems, and rigorous time management.

Johnson and Johnson R&D

CAD Design FSA / ISO Standards Verification and Validation

I worked on the next generation of orthopedic implants for J&J, leveraging CREO to design and test 3D models for surgeon usability, manufacturability, and patient comfort. Using verification and validation principles aligned with ISO standards and FDA regulations, I ensured design compliance and optimization. Additionally, I worked with injection-molded parts, redesigning them to account for material cooling kinetics, casting shape, and parting lines to reduce production costs, improve scalability, and elevate product quality.

Competition Robot

3D Design 3D Printing Laser Cutting Rapid Prototyping

This robot competed in a competition put on by the University of Michigan Mechanical Engineering Department. The design emphasized high maneuverability and durability in order to pick up and transport multiple cubes through an obstacle course. All parts were designed and manufactured within our team, meeting tolerance requirements in order to ensure proper fit and interaction of all subsystems and components.
